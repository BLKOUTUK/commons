Why this · 60 sec

Audre Lorde said it best —

we who were not meant to survive.

Five hundred years of racial capitalism

have tried to split us up.

To deny we exist or matter.

To divide and conquer —

Africans v Caribbeans,

Black men v Black women,

baby boomer v Gen Z —

anything to keep us busy

fighting each other,

and looking past the actual enemy.

● ● ●

In that context,

our relationship to each other

is itself revolutionary.

Black men loving Black men.

Black people loving Black people.

Black people retaining

the capacity for love at all.

● ● ●

But love can't only be courtly —

heart emojis, lol.

It has to be rooted in action.

It has to face today

and prepare for tomorrow.

It has to continue

to confound the racist.

The evidence will be our survival,

our Blackness,

our queerness,

our joy —

on land and online.
